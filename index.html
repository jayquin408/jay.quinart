About

From the heart of Silicon Valley, Jay Carlciyp Prado Quintos(1990, San Jose California, United States) is a digital media artist who works in many varieties of digital art methods such as computer animation, video art, game design, and interactive code art. He is exploring the world of 3D modelling in VR and in javascript coding. Most of his work is associated with his nostalgic childhood, which are drawn in by his reflection from reminiscent of his experience. As a child, being raised by his mother and with a father overseas, his life would be impacted by coloring books, video games, and picture books. Television was also exposed to him as he would interconnect his childhood life with animated cartoons to portray that nostalgic moment in time. To capture those moods as child is never lasting, so creating art in collaborating with his childhood memories are the basis of his work.

In his educational background, Jay Carlciyp Prado Quintos, attended Evergreen Valley College, where he completed his General Education, to be transferred to San Jose State University. At this institution he predominantly figure illustration and sculpting objects. Quintos also showcased his work in a juried art gallery in this institution and participate in finishing a mural for the institution.
